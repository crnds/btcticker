How it works. The Crypto Fear & Greed Index distils overall market sentiment into one daily number from 0 (extreme fear) to 100 (extreme greed), blending volatility, market momentum/volume, social media, market dominance and surveys. Low readings flag fear (often oversold); high readings flag greed (often overbought).

How it's fetched. The ticker pulls the latest reading from the keyless api.alternative.me/fng feed — the same source the Android home-screen widget uses — and caches it in localStorage (btcticker_v1_fng) for 12 hours, since the index only changes once a day. This dashboard paints the cached value instantly, then refreshes from the source in the background when the cache is older than 12 hours.
